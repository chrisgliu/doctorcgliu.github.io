My Interests

If you are asking what I would do if I was given millions of dollars and was told to go crazy, I would want to study biologically inspired computers. This includes Wetware Computers and Neuromorphic Chips. I believe that if we want something approaching human level intelligence, we need something approaching human level hardware.

I hope I don't accidentally help create an anime supercomputer that replaces the government.
