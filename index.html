Teaching philosophy

Fun: Why would you learn things that you don't enjoy?
● if you are not hungry, don't eat
● if you are not tired, don't sleep
● if you are not having fun, don't learn

Pride: Why would you do things that you are not proud of?
● you eat what you cook
● you respect what you fix
● you learn what you teach

Pragmatism: Practical Pedagogy
● Don't punish students for trying
  ○ Homework is participation
  ○ Feedback is provided with examples
  ○ Problems in exams will not be harder than homework
● Provide reward mechanisms for students
  ○ Free solutions to Homework in class
  ○ Free credit from quizzes with specific HW Problems
  ○ Free time with pre-recorded lectures
  ○ Students should compete at teaching to find gaps in knowledge (Free clout & other rewards maybe)
