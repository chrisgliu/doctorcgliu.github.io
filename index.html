About Me

My name is Chris. I am an AI CSE undergraduate student at The Ohio State University with an interest in ECE.

I view my career as something in between an eccentric hobby and an unhealthy obsession. I am someone who has fun learning and is genuinely interested in teaching. When it comes to PhDs, I think you have to be slightly crazy in order to be willing to study one topic for potentially seven years in hopes of solving one problem through research. Passion is for casuals.

I also like pie.



Research Interests

If you are asking what I would do if I was given millions of dollars and was told to go crazy, I would want to study biologically inspired AI. This would include Wetware Computers and Neuromorphic Chips. I believe that this is a Practical, Interdisciplinary, Environmental evolution for tech.

I hope I don't accidentally help create an anime supercomputer that replaces the government.



Teaching

I am someone who believes that you magically learn what you teach.

As a teacher, I would want to be as approachable as possible: sharing things I find interesting about a topic, encouraging students to tell me upfront if they don't understand, and rewarding students for showing up.

Pedagogy is fun.
