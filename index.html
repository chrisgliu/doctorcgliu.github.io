My Interests

If you are asking what I would do if I was given millions of dollars and was told to go crazy, I would want to research wetware computers. I think that a transition from binary signals to complex neural signals would be a natural evolution. I think that machine learning is an interesting tool for the design and optimization of circuits. I also think that applied machine learning in general is cool.

I hope I don't accidentally help create an anime supercomputer that replaces the government.
